The destination

What a Firm OS is

A system that runs quietly on top of the tools your team already lives in.

“

How they might be able to use AI to decrease cost and increase revenue.

— Josh Gibson, on the intro

A Firm OS is a Dodonai-hosted application that does four things. It collects your firm's context into one brain, a wiki you, your team, and your agents can query. It integrates with the software you already use, pushing and pulling data as needed. It runs custom workflow automation that replaces the tools and manual steps that no longer earn their place. And it gives you a visual dashboard plus automated notifications so you and your team stay in the loop. The design goal is to be as invisible as possible: it listens for incoming data, so people are not entering it by hand.

Pre-call briefing agent

Fifteen to thirty minutes before any external call, it posts a Slack brief: who you are meeting, their LinkedIn and company background, how the firm got connected, the agenda, and any open tasks or prior history. Zero setup per call.

Daily briefing agent

Pipeline & resurface agent

Encrypted + isolated

Encrypted in transit and at rest, in a database dedicated to your firm. Your data is not co-mingled with other customers.

We use zero-data-retention model vendors and do not train on your data. We will confirm this in writing.

Raw call transcripts and recordings auto-delete within roughly 48 hours. Only the reviewed, summarized notes are retained.
