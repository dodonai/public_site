15 min · Demo 1

Fax inbox triage: routing, alerts, AI response drafting

The outcome: the faxes and records that flood in each day land already sorted. The right case manager gets pinged on the right file. Nobody reads a stack that was never theirs.

▶What you will see the agent do, livestart here
→Ingest every inbound fax and the email-delivered records into one queue.
→OCR + classify each document: record type, provider, date of service.
→Match to the matter by client name, claim, and matter metadata, then file it into Smart Advocate.
→Alert the owner only: the responsible case manager gets pinged; the other six never see it.
→Draft the response where a routine reply is called for (records acknowledgment, follow-up request), queued for one-click approval.

Kills: all seven of you reading the same stack to find yours.

15 min · Demo 2

Email inbox management: prioritization and delegation

The outcome: a high-volume inbox handles itself. The agent reads it on a schedule, sorts and routes what belongs to the team, drafts the routine replies, and surfaces only what genuinely needs a person's eyes.

needs you

Tier 1 · surfaced

The few items that genuinely need the partner. The inbox collapses from "everything" to the three things that need a decision.

route

Tier 2 · delegated

Items that belong to a case manager get routed to them with the thread context attached, instead of sitting in one overloaded inbox.

handled

Tier 3 · drafted or filed

Routine replies written and queued for review. FYI items filed. The rest is dealt with without the partner touching it.

▶What you will see the agent do, liveyour #1 wish
→Reads the inbox on a schedule, not just when someone opens it.
→Prioritizes into three tiers: needs-you-now, route-to-the-team, FYI / handled.
→Delegates with context, so the right case manager gets the thread, not a bare forward.
→Drafts routine replies for review and surfaces only the few that truly need the partner.

Same engine as the fax triage: read → classify → route → draft → human gate. Prove one, prove both.

5 min · What stays yours

The agents remove the sorting, not the judgment

Automation stops where judgment starts. These steps stay with your people by design; the agents just hand them over clean and on time.

👤

Anything that leaves the firm

No demand, client letter, or email out goes without a person's review. The agents draft and queue; you approve. Nothing AI-written reaches a court or a client without sign-off.

🔀

Ambiguous matches

When the agent is not sure which matter a record belongs to, a person confirms. It never guesses into the wrong file.

⚖️

Legal judgment

Case strategy and what a record means for a claim stay with your attorneys. The agents assemble the inputs.

⌨️

You own the system

AI internal to your firm that you own. Not a subscription that disappears. The firm keeps the system and the data.

5 min · Built for regulated work

How PHI is handled

Medical records and client communications carry PHI and privilege. Here is how the platform handles them, and the answer to the hallucination concern.

✓ SOC 2 Certified ✓ HIPAA Compliant ✓ Tenant-resident ✓ BAA available
🔐

Your data stays yours

The agents run in your own tenant. Records and matter data are not pooled with anyone else's and are not used to train outside models. Encrypted in transit and at rest.

🏛️

Attorney in the loop on anything outbound

By design, nothing AI-drafted reaches a court, a client, or opposing counsel without an attorney's review. This is how the firm gets the speed without the hallucination risk.
