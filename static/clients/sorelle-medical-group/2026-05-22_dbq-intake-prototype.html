Veteran Intake & DBQ Generator

How this works. Clinician completes a short intake (~15 fields). AI infers the long-form clinical narratives and symptom indicators. The selected DBQ is auto-populated and ready for review & e-signature. Built for Sorelle's nexus-letter and DBQ workflow.
