Technical & access

QHow do you handle secure, unlimited-size record uploads?

Through Dodonai — it's built to ingest large medical-record sets with no 25 MB ceiling, on a HIPAA-compliant, SOC 2-certified platform. The system sends a secure per-matter upload link to the firm and files the records into the right matter folder automatically, no personal-folder sharing.

Quality control

QHow is Dodonai output verified against source PDFs?

Dodonai verifies its review against the source internally and traces back to it; the system surfaces the result for your review, and your verification gate stays in place. The human check is the feature — we make it quick, we don't remove it.

Quality control

QCan release be blocked until both approval and payment are complete?

Yes. The report won't release until the outstanding invoice clears and you've approved. If payment can't be confirmed, the OS holds and flags it — never auto-releases.
