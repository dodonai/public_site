Section 5

Phase 1 · pre-Nepal build 4 to 6 weeks after discovery

Bundled because the operational builds share data plumbing into Filevine.

Build A · Document Generation Engine

For the four templates. Pulls structured data from Filevine, drafts the prose-heavy sections (medical chronology, severe impairments, occupation analysis, legal arguments), and produces a Word draft your paralegal reviews and finalizes.

What changes: affidavit assembly drops from 1 to 2 hours to about 15 minutes of review. Demand letters and appeals: boilerplate plus client-specific prose drafted in one pass. Damages calc math runs automatically from policy and payment data.

Build B · Support Inbox Triage Agent

Reads incoming support email, classifies (new client, existing client, vendor, junk, urgent deadline), and routes to the right one or two people instead of blasting all ten.

What changes: client emails surface inside hours, not days. Paralegals get back the attention they were spending on triage.

Build C · Cognito Forms to Filevine Bridge

Questionnaire data clients submit through Cognito flows directly into the right Filevine fields, ready for merge.

What changes: a person-day of data entry per week, returned. Cleaner data downstream.

Build D · Nick's Personal Inbox Agent

Same triage model as Build B but scoped to Nick's personal inbox. Surfaces what needs Nick directly, defers what's better handled by the team, drafts replies on the recurring patterns.

What changes: Nick gets the rainmaker focus he described on Monday. The inbox stops being a clearinghouse of everything; it becomes a list of what only Nick can decide.
