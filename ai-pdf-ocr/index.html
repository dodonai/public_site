How is Dodonai's OCR different?

Dodonai OCR doesn't just extract text; it understands context, transcribes handwriting accurately, identifies and formats tables clearly, and provides meaningful descriptions of images. Why is legacy OCR insufficient?

Legacy OCR technologies struggle with complex documents, handwritten notes, varying font styles, and poor-quality scans, often resulting in inaccuracies, manual rework, and significant delays. They lack contextual understanding, meaning they cannot reliably differentiate text from tables or interpret image content. These limitations make legacy OCR solutions costly, inefficient, and inadequate for modern document processing demands.
