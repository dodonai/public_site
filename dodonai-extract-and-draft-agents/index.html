Dodonai XD: AI Intelligent Document Processing and Report Generation

What is AI Intelligent Document Processing and Report Generation?

Dodonai XD simplifies document processing and report creation. Create reusable templates that specify the data type and format you need. Dodonai's advanced AI seamlessly extracts, organizes, and formats data from any combination of source documents.

How Can I Use Dodonai XD?

Legal: Extract and record data from common forms and contracts, organize discovery responses and objections, extract and organize evidence from discovery documents, and much more.
Health: Extract common data from medical records and automate  medical record reviews.
Insurance: Rapidly process and compile comprehensive claim reports, policy analyses, and underwriting documents, streamlining operations and reducing manual effort.
Business Analysis: Swiftly develop detailed, precise analysis reports and business intelligence summaries, enabling more strategic decision-making.
Operations: Automate routine operational and performance reporting, improving accuracy, consistency, and overall productivity.

How is Dodonai's OCR different?

Dodonai intelligently automates data extraction, sorting, and formatting through user-defined templates, significantly reducing manual reporting time. It uniquely allows interactive adjustments post-generation, ensuring precise final reports.

Why switch to AI-driven document processing?

Manual processing is error-prone, repetitive, and time-consuming. Traditional intelligent document processing is too expensive for most tasks. Dodonai eliminates these inefficiencies by automating complex data handling tasks, drastically increasing productivity and accuracy, at a cost that is appealing for automating most document tasks.
